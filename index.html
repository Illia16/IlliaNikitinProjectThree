Visual meal generator

How to use?

The visual meal generator allows user to craft meals by adding food items from the ingridient section to the table by clicking button "add". Once something is made, there's a notification about what was cooked.

On the right side of the screen in the Cooked meals section, there's a list of cooked meals and information on what ingridients needed to cook certain meal. Once something is cooked, there's visual respesentation on the meal that was cooked as well as a counter on how many times it's been cooked.

Down below, there's Add new ingridient(s) and meal section where user can add any ingridients they want to the database. Also, there's a possibility of addding user's own meal with any amount of ingridients. The added ingridiens/meals are displayed dynamically once added.

For example, let's try making TACO. First, we gotta check on the right what ingredients do we need. Then, see if they are availble in the list of ingridients. If not, we need to add each ingridient using the form below. Once, they are all added, add the needed ingridients on the table. Once they're all there, our TACO is made.
